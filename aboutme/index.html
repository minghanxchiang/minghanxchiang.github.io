About Me

Hi, I am Daniel Chiang. I am a data analyst in a tech start-up in Taipei. I had been conducting quantitive economic research using experimental methods and statistical causal models for over 5 years before I left academia. My greatest enthusiasm in my career is to apply suitable statistical models on the right dataset to answer real-world questions and gain valuable insights.

This blog would be the place where I store my study notes, showcase my previous projects, and most importantly, share my experience of employing applied economics research methods to solve business problems.

Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or Letterboxd if you’re also a huge movie fan. As a gourmand wannabe, I also have a Medium Page (in Chinese) that posts some of my greatest journeies to some cool restaurants and bars.
